Esthetique

Training Academy

Exclusive distributor of Princess Lift (First Lift ) PDO threads in UK.

We provide high quality hands-on training to Aesthetic Practitioners in our training facility in Central London, as well as in house.

Courses

The course is one day session and includes treatments with threads: straight, screw (spiral), barbed threads.

  • Upper face (forehead lines, Glabella, eyebrows strengthening, crow’s feet)
  • Mid face ( nasolabial folds, cheeks lifting, sagging skin on lower cheeks, marionette lines, smoker’s lines)
  • Lower face (jaw line, second chin)
  • Neck and décolletage

The course will include advance techniques using straight, screw and 4D Barbs on face and body:

  • Eye brow lift and Eye brow tightening
  • Jaw line , second chin and neck with 4D barbs
  • Body parts: upper arms, abdomen, tights and buttocks

Princes Lift LFL PDO Threads Training Courses are hands-on with live demonstration to give you the maximum opportunity to gain experience and confidence

At the end of each training, you will receive:

  • Starter pack, with threads which allows you to treat first 2-3 patients
  • Training manual

We also provide post training care:

  • One mentoring session (up to 4 patients to be treated) in your office
  • We are always available to answer any questions you may have in a future

The training will be conducted by the certified trainer- Medical Doctor, who spends quality time to show various techniques and methods of applying threads on face and neck during the training.

Esthetique Distribution Ltd provides also bespoke trainings in your premises.

Please contact: iinfo@pdo-threads.co.uk for further information and quotation.
