About ESTHETIQUE

DISTRIBUTION IS AN EXCLUSIVE DISTRIBUTOR OF FIRST LIFT LFL PDO THREADS IN THE UK. FIRST LIFT LFL is premium lifting thread made of PDO (Polydioxanone) which is absorbed after proliferating collagen by stimulating fibroblast. Any of you can experience overall face contouring, skin elasticity. It brings natural and dramatic effect to face and body after simple and safe treatment. The superior quality of the Ultra Thin Wall Needle allows the treatment easy, atraumatic and painless.

PDO is a strong synthetic monofilament fiber, consisting of one or two twisted solid fiber thread, which dissolves in the tissue after a certain time.

Polydioxanone material more 30 years is used in plastic surgery, gastroenterology, urology, gyneacology, ophthalmology for cutaneous and subcutaneous sutures.

This material meets all the requirements established by the United States Pharmacopeia (USP) for surgical sutures.

  • Very high tensile strength
  • Excellent pliability
  • Smooth tissue passage
