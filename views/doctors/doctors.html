FIRST LIFT LFL

is a premium selection of most technologically advanced second generation PDO THREADS (straight, spiral (screw), barbs and 4D barbs) resulting in meeting requirements of most demanding patients.

ELIMINATE FINE WRINKLES

  • Smoother the deep wrinkles
  • Improve skin colour, elasticity, texture
  • Delay aging process and prevent sagging

INDICATION

  • Recommended for the prevention and treatment of involution changes due to aging
  • Patients age group 30-55

APPLICATION ON THE FACE

  • Vertical and horizontal forehead wrinkle
  • Brow lifting, lower the outer edges of the eyebrows, flabby, soft tissue drooping eyebrows
  • Lower eyelid – lagged bag
  • Malar prominence
  • Pronounced naso-labial fold
  • Malar-jugal groove
  • Submental fold
  • Marionette fold
  • V-Lifting
  • Drooping cheeks and the area under the lower jaw
  • The folds of skin on the chin
  • Wrinkles cheeks

APPLICATION ON THE BODY

  • Folds end wrinkles of skin on the neck and chest
  • Breast and bottoms firming, shaping
  • Sagging tissue end stretch marks on the buttocks, abdomen, arms and legs
  • Rugged terrain skin and subcutaneous tissue (especially after liposuction)
  • Obesity and cellulite – arms, abdomen, legs, ribs
  • Alopecia

CONTRADICTIONS

  • Various acute infectious diseases (SARS, influenza, etc.)
  • Inflammation of the skin
  • Cancers
  • Tendency to keloids
  • Bleeding tendency and some diseases of the blood (blood clotting)
